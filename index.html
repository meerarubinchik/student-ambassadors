Our Vision



To create a welcoming and informative environment for prospective students and community members. Student Ambassadors demonstrate professionalism, dedication, and knowledge of the School's programs and services that contribute to the mission and vision of the School of Computing and Information.

Meera Rubinchik

Hi! My name is Meera, and I’m currently in my Junior year working toward a degree under the User Centered Design focus of Information Science. I’m interested in the front end design aspect of web development and have been recently working with graphic design and animations.
